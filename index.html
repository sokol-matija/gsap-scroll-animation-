Discover Zagreb

A Scroll-Driven Journey Through Croatia's Capital

The City of a Million Hearts

Zagreb Cathedral

A towering masterpiece of Gothic architecture, the cathedral is a symbol of the city. Its twin spires reach for the sky, a testament to centuries of history and resilience.

St. Mark's Church

Famous for its colorful tiled roof depicting the coats of arms of Croatia, Dalmatia, and Slavonia, and the emblem of Zagreb.

Lotrščak Tower

For over a hundred years, the Grič cannon has been fired from this tower to mark midday. The tower offers stunning panoramic views of the city.

Croatian National Theatre

A masterpiece of architecture and art, the theatre is a central institution of Croatian culture. The building itself is a spectacle of neo-baroque design.

Museum of Broken Relationships

A unique and poignant museum that showcases personal objects and stories of past relationships. It's a testament to the universality of love and loss.

Zagreb Funicular

One of the shortest funiculars in the world, it connects the Lower Town (Donji Grad) with the Upper Town (Gornji Grad). A charming and historic ride.

Dolac Market

The bustling heart of the city, Dolac Market is a vibrant open-air market filled with fresh produce, local delicacies, and the iconic red umbrellas.

Zagreb Eye

An observation deck offering a breathtaking 360° view of the city. A perfect spot to appreciate the beauty of Zagreb from above.

Nikola Tesla Technical Museum

A fascinating museum dedicated to the genius inventor Nikola Tesla and the history of technology. A place of innovation and discovery.

Maksimir Park

A vast and beautiful park, home to lakes, forests, and the Zagreb Zoo. A perfect escape into nature within the city.
